The Project.

Resilient Smart Gardens is a step towards sustainable systems. Our goal at RSG is to promote environmentalism, encourage a green thumb with smart gardening tactics, and conserve water. A Smart Garden is an embedded system to accompany any garden bed - providing its users the necessary data analysis tools to help their garden grow without depleting their resources. California's semi-permanent dry season has been taking a toll on its inhabitants - attempting to encourage a statewide effort to save water. But with Resilient Smart Gardens, our goal is to give your garden a chance. Worry not about your water usage. Choose Smart, and let Smart Gardens handle it for you.
